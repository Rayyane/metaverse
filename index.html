Our Mining Strategy

Bitcoin and crypto mining is like real estate, the best time to invest was 10 years ago, the second best time to invest is now. Mining is an ever evolving business and incredibly competitive. MetaMiner will implement a Profit Switching strategy - effectively this is a way to maximize your rigs profitability by switching between coins to mine depending on whichever is the most profitable at a given point of time.
We source, purchase, and supply the newest-generation miners that are optimized to get MetaMiner NFT holders best-in-class hash rate with high energy efficiency to minimize costs and maximize your mining power.

Our operation will mine Bitcoin and altcoins, algorithmically switching between the most profitable, and pointing our hash in that direction during peak profitability. At the end of each day mining revenue is converted into $ETH and injected into the MetaMiner ecosystem.
